Fried Rice

Savor the layers of tender noodles, savory meat sauce, creamy ricotta, and melted cheese in this homemade lasagna. With aromatic garlic and a rich marinara sauce, it's a comforting and flavorful dish that will please everyone at the table.

Ingredients

  • Lasagna noodles
  • Ground beef
  • Garlic
  • Marinara sauce
  • Cheese
  • Seasoning

Steps

  1. Cook lasagna noodles according to package instructions, then set aside.
  2. In a skillet, brown ground beef or Italian sausage with minced garlic. Drain excess grease.
  3. Add marinara sauce and seasonings to the skillet, simmer for a few minutes.
  4. Layer sauce, noodles, ricotta cheese, mozzarella cheese, and Parmesan cheese in a baking dish, repeating until ingredients are used.
  5. Cover with foil and bake at 375°F (190°C) for 25 minutes, then remove foil and bake for an additional 10-15 minutes until cheese is melted and bubbly.
  6. Let it rest before serving.
