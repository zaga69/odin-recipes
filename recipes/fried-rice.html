Fried Rice

Whip up this tasty fried rice dish with fluffy rice, vegetables, eggs, and savory soy sauce. It's a versatile and satisfying meal that can be customized to your liking.

Ingredients

  • Rice
  • Oil
  • Garlic
  • Eggs
  • Soy sauce

Steps

  1. Heat oil in a large skillet or wok.
  2. Add minced garlic to the skillet and cook for about 30 seconds until fragrant.
  3. Push the garlic to one side and pour the lightly beaten eggs into the empty space. Scramble the eggs until cooked through.
  4. Add the cooked rice to the skillet and stir-fry for a few minutes, breaking up any clumps.
  5. Drizzle soy sauce over the rice and continue to stir-fry for another minute.
  6. Remove from heat and serve hot.
